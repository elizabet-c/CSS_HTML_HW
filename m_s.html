    SPF

    Although SPF can be notoriously annoying to apply as many come with an unpleasant smell or uncomfortable feeling on the skin, SPF is extremely important to keep skin looking healthy. At the least, I encourage SPF be worn on days with more-than-normal sun exposure. However, if you find an SPF that makes your skin feel amazing, then this can become a daily addition to your morning skincare routine. SPF can be categorized as either chemical, physical or a combination. Both are effective. Chemical absorbs the UV rays and works it magic to minimize the harm caused by UV exposure. Chemical sunscreens are much less likely to cause a white-cast than physical sunscreens. Physical sunscreens often are comprised of titanium or zinc oxide.
